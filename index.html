Features

Everything for a smarter night out

From real-time BAC monitoring to social events, SipWise has everything you need to enjoy responsibly.

Real-Time BAC

Watch your BAC rise and fall in real-time with our signature frosted pint glass visualisation. Physics-based liquid simulation with colour-coded safety levels.

Smart Logging

Log drinks in seconds with smart presets for beer, wine, spirits, and cocktails. Track prices, snap photos, and tag venues automatically.

Social Events

Create live group drinking events and track everyone's progress in real-time. Share invite codes, see who's out, and keep your group connected.

Safety First

Hydration reminders, sober-up countdown timers, hangover predictions, and emergency contacts. Because a great night means getting home safe.
